What is Healthcare Heroes?

One email. One message worth sharing.

Healthcare Heroes is a peer-to-peer health movement in Weymouth & Portland. Every month, a small group of local people get one simple, human message about health and wellbeing — and share it with the people they care about.

No jargon. No appointments. No pressure. Just a small kindness, passed from one person to another.

That's it. That's the whole idea.

Did you know? People are more likely to seek help when someone they trust tells them about it — not when they see a poster or an advert. One conversation from someone who cares is worth more than a hundred leaflets.
This Month's Mission

Tell one person about Healthcare Heroes.

This March, we're not sending health tips. We're asking our Heroes to do something simpler — and more powerful.

Think of one person in your life who might benefit from being part of this. A neighbour. A colleague. Someone you've been meaning to check in on. Then tell them about it.

You don't need a speech. You just need a sentence.

Try saying

"I get a monthly email with simple health tips I can share with people I care about. It's free, it's local, and it takes about a minute. Want me to add you?"

If they say yes, send them this page. If they want to join, there's a button at the bottom. That's the whole mission.

Pass it on

Share this page with someone you think would care.

The simplest thing you can do right now. Send this link to one person. That's a Hero move.
